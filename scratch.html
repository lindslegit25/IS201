Health and Wellness

Wellness Tips

Nutrition

  • Consume 3-5 servings of fruits & or vegetables per day
  • Make 1/2 your grains whole
  • Limit Saturated Fats, Sugar, and Sodium

Learn About the Choose My Plate Dietary Guidelines

Exercise

  • Aerobic Exercise: 150 min/wk of moderate-intensity, 75 min/wk of vigorous-intensity
  • Strength training: exercise all 6 major muscle group 2x per week:
    1. Abs
    2. Chest
    3. Back
    4. Shoulders
    5. Arms
    6. Legs

Stress Management

  • Practice deep breathing/meditation
  • Sleep 7-9 hrs/night
  • Exercise—even a short walk—releases endorphins and helps reduce stress
